The Rock, Becky Lynch and More Come To Brawlhalla In SummerSlam In-Game Event

Ubisoft’s platform fighter has hit the ring. Brawlhalla is a free to play fighting game in the vein of Nintendo’s Super Smash Bros. Instead of Mario and Link, the combatants include original fantasy warriors, Ubisoft’s Rayman, and Finn from Adventure Time. As of today, you can add WWE wrestlers to that list.

John Cena, The Rock, Becky Lynch, and Xavier Woods (with some New Day assistance) will come to the game as “Epic Crossover” skins for four existing heroes. Not only do the existing characters get a new coat of paint, but each signature attack gets a dose of WWE flair. Whether it’s The Rock summoning a flaming bull or John Cena bouncing off the ropes, you’ll easily pin your opponents online for the 1, 2, 3.

Each skin is available individually for 300 Mammoth Coins in the in-game shop. For a real-world money comparison, you can purchase 340 Mammoth Coins for $13, making skins hover around the $10 range. For those who don’t want to spend money, you can take your existing Brawlhalla characters into the free Brawldown game mode. This limited-time offering includes a ring, breakable tables, and fun rope physics.
