The Powder Jar (Japanese: こないれ Powder Container) is a Key Item introduced in Generation III.

Effect

Stores up to 99,999 units of Berry Powder. Berry Powder is produced by the Berry Crush minigame in Pokémon FireRed, LeafGreen, and Emerald; the player must have a Powder Jar in order to participate in Berry Crush.
