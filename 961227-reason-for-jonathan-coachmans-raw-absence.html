Reason For Jonathan Coachman’s RAW Absence

David Otunga subbed in for Jonathan Coachman on commentary on RAW tonight, and Michael Cole said Coachman was ‘on assigment.’

Coachman is currently in Atlantic City, New Jersey doing coverage of the World Long Drive golf championships; he noted that this is the fourth and final event he will cover that required him to miss RAW.
