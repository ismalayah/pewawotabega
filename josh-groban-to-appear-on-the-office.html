Josh Groban to Appear on The Office

Singer Josh Groban is coming to Dunder-Mifflin Sabre Scranton.

He will play Andy Bernard’s brother (!) during an episode in the upcoming season eight, when the whole ‘Nard Dog family comes for a visit.

7th Heaven patriarch Stephen Collins and ET mom Dee Wallace will play the parents of Ed Helms’ character (and also Groban’s) this fall.
