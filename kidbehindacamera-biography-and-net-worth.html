KidBehindACamera Biography And Net Worth

KidBehindACamera is a famous American Youtuber, cameraman and who is best known as the creator of the super hit web series ‘The Angry Grandpa Show’

KidBehindACamera started his Youtube career in 2007 where he began posting the Angry Grandpa videos which featured his parents as the main characters. The channel was later suspended for violating the terms of the platform.

KidBehindACamera later launched his channel ‘TheAngryGrandpaShow’ along with his father Charles Green in 2010 and posted several videos afterward. He currently has over 2.5 million subscribers on his channel.
