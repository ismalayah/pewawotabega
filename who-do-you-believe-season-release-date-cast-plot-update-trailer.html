Who Do You Believe Season 2: Release Date, Cast, Plot, Trailer And Everything That You Want To Know!

The most interesting detail about mystery shows is how you don’t see it coming. You will be rooting for one character, even eliminating the possibility of them being guilty. Then it all starts to make sense, and you realize you are not a good judge of nature as you thought. The other detail is their unpredictability as compared to the sappy rom-com. Showing on ABC is the Who Do You Believe drama series, a crime series different from your typical type. It has one of the unique ways of presenting the story making it stand out among others. You are taken through both sides of the case and then left to judge who you think is telling the truth. With the viewers loving playing the detectives in the series, do we get the second installment shortly? Stick around to find out.

Who Do You Believe Season 2 Release Date: When will it air?

It will be quite long before we get a specific release date for season two. The first season’s pilot premiered on May 3rd, and since then, there have been only a total of two episodes out and streaming. The rest are expected to drop weekly until the eleventh episode, which might be ready sometime in July.

Different factors have to be considered for its renewal, and the biggest determinant so far is ratings for the show’s rating is a downtrend, which is always not a good thing. The show has a rating of 4.4/10 on IMDB, with many viewers expressing their sentiments about how they do not love the show. This might be the sign we need to make peace with that we may never get a second season. However, it is too early, and we will have to wait for the official updates from the showrunners.

Who Do You Believe Season 2 Plot: What should we expect?

The series gives two sides to the story, and the viewer is left to be the detective; Discover the truth and the lie. After they are taken through and have listened to the account of the two narratives, they are left with the mandate to decide who they think is genuine. Since the audience plays detective, they are expected to step in the shoes of the narrators and rightly deduce by weighing facts, just like a detective would have. The most interesting bit is that there are no limits o the lengths one could go to present matters they think are true irrespective of the subject. After listening to their accounts, Who Do You Believe is the question that lingers in every audience’s mind.

Who Do You Believe Season 2 Cast: Who will be in?

The principal cast of the series includes Desi Louise as Charity, Donna Geek as Gopher Mom, Jessi Sims as Charity’s daughter, James Espinosa as APS Officer, Kenneth JP Morgan as Kyle Johnson, and Destini Renee as an APS officer. A renewal of the series might see all of the casts reprising their roles. We should also expect new faces.

Who Do You Believe Season 2 Trailer: When can we watch it?

The crime drama season one is yet to be fully released, and till it is all out, the chances of a season two trailer are very low. The last episode is expected o drop on July 12th. If you have not binged on the series, it premieres every week on ABC.
