Huge Spoiler On WWEs Plan For Matt Cardona &amp; Chelsea Green

Matt Cardona, formerly known as Zack Ryder during his tenure in WWE, struggled to gain significant screen time and was unable to reach his full potential within the company. He was eventually released by WWE in 2020 and went on to achieve greater success outside the organization. That being said, WWE seems to have big plans for both him and his wife, Chelsea Green.

Ringside News also exclusively reported that Matt Cardona is on WWE’s radar for a return. To the surprise of no one, fans have already started speculating if he will end up in WWE again or not. Cardona has made sure fans keep thinking about him.

In fact, even his wife Chelsea Green has been in the news for her return to WWE. As previously reported, Chelsea Green already re-signed with WWE a while ago.

According to a report by Sean Ross Sapp behind Fightful’s paywall, it was noted that WWE has plans for Chelsea Green to portray a comedic “Karen” type character.

We haven’t been told if Chelsea Green is being planned for the Royal Rumble, but there have been tentative creative plans for her to play a comedic “Karen” type character based on what we heard. She’s actually been signed for months.

In addition to that, Sean Ross Sapp directly asked Matt Cardona if he will be appearing at the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event. Cardona made it clear he had no intention of giving a straight answer.

We flat out asked Matt Cardona if he was coming in for the WWE Royal Rumble, and he said “In San Antonio the day before the Rumble? What am I, a mark?

We will have to wait and see whether Matt Cardona and Chelsea Green will make their return to the company during the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event this week.
